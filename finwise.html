🌱 Investing Basics

Compound Interest: The 8th Wonder

The Formula
A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)
💡 Investing $200/month from age 22 → $98,740 by age 40
Compound interest means you earn interest on your interest. It is the most powerful force in investing. The earlier you start, the less money you actually have to put in to reach your goals.
⚡ Starting just 10 years earlier could give you $60,000 more by retirement, even if you invest less total cash.
